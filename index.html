Regulatory Foundation

Built on CBN law.
Not opinion.

Every enforcement action ForgeFlow takes is backed by a specific regulatory instrument. This is not a complaint tool — it's a compliance engine.

CBN Circular FPR/DIR/GEN/CIR/06/010

Mandates resolution of failed PoS and Web/Internet transactions within 72 hours. ForgeFlow enforces this automatically for every applicable case.

Web & PoS Transactions · 72-hour window

CBN Consumer Protection Framework 2022

Establishes 48-hour resolution requirement for ATM and USSD transaction failures. ForgeFlow tracks this timeline separately per transaction type.

ATM & USSD Transactions · 48-hour window

CBN Complaint Management Framework 2021

Requires banks to maintain a Complaint Management Officer (CMO) and provide written resolution confirmations. ForgeFlow demands this in every escalation letter.

Formal complaint procedure · CMO accountability

Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023 (NDPA)

ForgeFlow collects explicit consent before processing any personal data, maintains a tamper-evident consent log, and stores all data within Nigeria's jurisdiction.

NDPA §2.1 · Explicit consent · Data sovereignty
